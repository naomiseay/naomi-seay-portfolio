Process Improvement Annotations
Improvement 1 — Credit Check Automation
Credit verification (Finance lane, Decision 1) was previously a manual 4-hour process. Automated credit scoring integration reduced this to under 15 minutes, eliminating the primary bottleneck at order intake.
Improvement 2 — Pack & QA Checkpoint
A dedicated QA check step was inserted at the Pack stage after analysis showed 68% of order errors originated post-pick. This single change reduced the overall error rate from 2.1% to 0.8%.
Improvement 3 — Parallel Invoice Issuance
Invoice generation was decoupled from the fulfillment confirmation step and now runs in parallel with carrier handoff, reducing Days Sales Outstanding by 1.2 days on average.
