This year's results reflect five years of community investment, partnership-building, and the unwavering commitment of our staff, volunteers, and donors. Our 2025 cohort achieved the highest graduation and post-secondary enrollment rates in Horizon's history — a direct result of earlier, more sustained intervention. As we enter 2026, our expansion into the Northside and Westfield neighborhoods will bring these outcomes to 400 additional young people.
