🌐 About My Translation Work

I am a native Vietnamese speaker with professional written English. My translation workflow combines native fluency with AI-assisted MTPE (machine translation post-editing): AI produces a fast first draft, I review and refine for tone, cultural nuance, and accuracy. The result is professional-quality bilingual content delivered significantly faster than manual translation alone.

📄 Sample 1 — Formal Business (English → Vietnamese)

Sample 01 Business Formal
🇺🇸 English Original
Dear Mr. Nguyen,

Thank you for your inquiry regarding our data management services. We would be pleased to schedule a consultation at your earliest convenience to discuss how our team can support your organization's needs.

Please feel free to contact us at any time.

Best regards,
Naomi Seay
🇻🇳 Vietnamese Translation
Kính gửi anh Nguyễn,

Cảm ơn anh ðã liên hệ và hỏi thăm về dịch vụ quản lý dữ liệu của chúng tôi. Chúng tôi rất vui ðược sắp xếp một buổi tư vấn vào thời gian thuận tiện nhất cho anh ðể thảo luận về cách ðội ngũ của chúng tôi có thể hỗ trợ nhu cầu của tổ chức.

Xin vui lòng liên hệ với chúng tôi bất cứ lúc nào.

Trân trọng,
Naomi Seay
Translator note: Formal Vietnamese uses specific honorifics ("Kính gửi", "Trân trọng") that don't have word-for-word English equivalents — they signal professional respect in a culturally appropriate way. Literal translation would sound unnatural to a Vietnamese reader.

💬 Sample 2 — Customer-Facing / Conversational (Vietnamese → English)

Sample 02 Conversational
🇻🇳 Vietnamese Original
Tôi muốn hỏi về lịch hẹn của tôi vào tuần tới. Tôi có thể dời lịch sang ngày thứ Tư ðược không? Tôi cần ðiều chỉnh vì có việc ðột xuất gia ðình.
🇺🇸 English Translation
I would like to ask about my appointment next week. Is it possible to reschedule to Wednesday? I need to adjust due to an unexpected family matter.
Translator note: The Vietnamese original uses informal but polite register — common in service contexts. The English translation preserves the polite, non-urgent tone appropriate for a customer service interaction without over-formalizing it.

⚙ Sample 3 — Technical / Instructional (English → Vietnamese)

Sample 03 Technical
🇺🇸 English Original
Data entry associates are responsible for inputting client records accurately into the system. Each entry must be reviewed for completeness before submission. Any discrepancies should be flagged immediately using the error-reporting form located in the shared drive.
🇻🇳 Vietnamese Translation
Nhân viên nhập liệu có trách nhiệm nhập thông tin hồ sơ khách hàng vào hệ thống một cách chính xác. Mỗi mục nhập phải ðược kiểm tra ðầy ðủ trước khi gửi. Bất kỳ sai lệch nào cũng cần ðược báo cáo ngay lập tức thông qua mẫu báo cáo lỗi trong thư mục dùng chung.
Translator note: Technical Vietnamese uses specific terminology for business processes. "Nhập liệu" (data entry), "hồ sơ" (records), and "sai lệch" (discrepancies) are standard professional terms — not literal word-for-word translations — ensuring the document reads naturally to a Vietnamese professional audience.

🔁 My Translation Process

1
First read
Understand the full context and intent — not just the words. Identify tone, audience, and register before typing anything.
2
Draft translation
Translate meaning, not just words. Match register and cultural tone. Use MTPE for speed on longer pieces.
3
Back-check
Re-read both versions to catch unnatural phrasing, missed nuance, or idioms that don't transfer cleanly.
4
Final review
Confirm terminology consistency and cultural appropriateness. Would a native speaker find this natural?
