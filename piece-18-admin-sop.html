1. Purpose & Scope

This Standard Operating Procedure defines the end-to-end process for onboarding new clients to the remote administrative operations framework. It establishes consistent, repeatable steps to ensure a professional client experience, accurate record creation, and proper system provisioning across all onboarding scenarios.

This SOP applies to all administrative staff and operations coordinators responsible for client intake, account setup, and welcome communications. It covers the full onboarding lifecycle from signed agreement through system access confirmation.

2. Roles & Responsibilities

Role Responsibility Owner
Admin Coordinator Primary contact for client during onboarding. Manages intake forms, CRM entry, and system provisioning. Administrative Team
Operations Lead Reviews and approves all onboarding records. Handles escalations and exceptions to standard process. Operations Management
IT / Systems Admin Provisions system access, creates client accounts, and confirms credential delivery. IT Department
Billing Coordinator Creates client billing profile, confirms payment method on file, and issues initial invoice. Finance Team
Account Manager Conducts welcome call, sets expectations for ongoing service delivery, and closes onboarding process. Client Success Team

3. Step-by-Step Procedure

1
Receive Signed Agreement
Confirm receipt of executed client agreement via DocuSign or equivalent. Verify all required signature fields are complete.
  • Download executed PDF to designated client folder in shared drive
  • Log agreement receipt timestamp in CRM with case number
  • Send auto-acknowledgment email to client using Template OB-01
2
Create Client Record in CRM
Establish the master client record as the single source of truth for all subsequent onboarding tasks.
  • Complete all required fields: company name, primary contact, billing contact, service tier, contract start date
  • Assign unique Client ID using format CLI-YYYY-XXXX
  • Tag account with relevant service categories and account manager assignment
3
Initiate Systems Provisioning Request
Submit IT provisioning ticket within 4 business hours of CRM record creation.
  • Complete System Access Request Form (IT-REQ-003) in ticketing system
  • Specify access tier per contract: Standard / Advanced / Enterprise
  • Include primary contact email and phone for credential delivery confirmation
4
Set Up Billing Profile
Coordinate with Finance to create billing profile before system access goes live.
  • Forward signed agreement to billing@company.com with subject: NEW CLIENT — [Client Name] — [Contract Date]
  • Confirm payment terms: Net 30 / Net 15 / Prepaid as specified in agreement
  • Verify invoice delivery address matches contract
5
Send Welcome Package
Deliver welcome communications within 1 business day of CRM record completion.
  • Send welcome email using Template OB-02 (includes service overview, key contacts, escalation path)
  • Share Getting Started Guide PDF and applicable user documentation links
  • Schedule welcome call — 30 minutes with Account Manager within 5 business days
6
Confirm System Access Delivery
Verify that client has received and successfully accessed all provisioned systems.
  • Follow up with IT after 24 hours if credential delivery not confirmed
  • Send access confirmation check-in to client using Template OB-03
  • Document any access issues in CRM case log with resolution status
7
Conduct Welcome Call
Account Manager completes introductory call, sets service expectations, and answers initial questions.
  • Review service scope, SLA commitments, and escalation contacts with client
  • Confirm primary day-to-day contacts on client side
  • Document call notes in CRM and share meeting summary within 24 hours
8
Close Onboarding Case
Confirm all onboarding tasks are complete and formally transition account to ongoing service delivery mode.
  • Verify all checklist items (Section 4) are marked complete
  • Update CRM account status from "Onboarding" to "Active"
  • Send onboarding completion confirmation to client and internal team

4. Onboarding Checklist

✓
Signed agreement received and filed
✓
CRM client record created with all required fields
✓
IT provisioning ticket submitted
✓

✓
Welcome package sent (email + documentation)
System access confirmed by client
Welcome call completed and notes documented
Account status updated to Active in CRM
Onboarding case closed and archived
