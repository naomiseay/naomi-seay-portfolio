🌟 Project Context

Goal
Drive appointment bookings and build local brand presence for a nail salon
Audience
Local Cincinnati women, 22–45, interested in self-care and beauty services
Deliverables
7-day calendar, 5 designed post briefs, caption copy, CTA for each post

This package demonstrates end-to-end social media content creation: strategy, copy, design direction, and scheduling guidance. Every caption has a single clear CTA; the mix balances educational, social proof, and promotional content (no more than 2 promo posts per week).

💡 Content Principles Applied

  • 🎯 One CTA per post. Every post has a single, clear call to action. No competing asks. This is the most common mistake small business social media makes — and the easiest to fix.
  • ⚖ The 80/20 content rule. No more than 2 promotional posts per 7-day cycle. 5 posts build value, trust, and community. 2 posts sell. Audiences unfollow accounts that only push sales.
  • 💬 Human captions, not corporate copy. Emojis are used intentionally to break up text and match platform tone. No industry jargon. Reads like a person, not a brand department.
  • 🎨 Consistent visual identity. Same color palette, fonts, and logo placement across all 5 designed posts. Brand recognition builds through repetition — not variety.
